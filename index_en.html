Ciro's Farmhouse

It is located nearby the village of Vita, in Contrada Auro Roccone, on the provincial road number 44 between Makani's forest and the highest part of the historical center of Vita as far as 50 meters from the intersection with Via Grazia and farm road for Contrada Makani.

At just over 500 meters of altitude it offers a panoramic allocation over the territory of the province of Trapani and Palermo, in the middle of Trapani's province; It is 35 km away from Trapani, approximately the same distance from the other major municipalities of the territory: Alcamo, Castelvetrano, Marsala, 45Km from Mazara del Vallo; 7km from Salemi; 90Km from Palermo.

The location offers a panoramic view together with a quiet and peaceful environment.

Services

Equipped with very comfortable single, double, triple and quadruple bedrooms, access for disabled, restaurant, pool, secured parking, bicycles for adults and children, children's playground, table tennis, floor mats and other material for gym exercises. Each room has its own bathroom with shower, toilet and sink and is also equipped with TV and free wi-fi access. There is also a single bedroom with its own bathroom with tub, toilet, sink and bidet, but outside and next the room.

There is also an area usable for the celebration* of marriages and civil unions, for information call (+39) 351-8845667.

Typical Mediterranean Cuisine. Meals prepared with local and organic products, focusing first of all on the freshness and genuineness of the ingredients: fresh pasta, Rice timballi, arancine, meat and fish couscous and vegetables' tabbouleh. Huge availability and variety of fruits and vegetables, legumes, meats, oily fish and wines from our own private wine cellar or from local farmers and breeders; we favor the use of raw olive oil on our dishes. Upon request and booking, privileged menus can be arranged that can satisfy the most exigent requests, with all variety of fish based menus, depending on market availability. Also available dishes for celiac and allergy sufferers.

You can participate in the typical Sicilian cooking school by reservation. It's really funny!

Typical Sicilian cakes and desserts (upon request other types of cakes and deserts can be created).

We organize events: pool parties with entertainment, birthdays, anniversaries, holy communions, confirmations, graduations etc.

Your stay

Surrounded by a centenary olive plantation, a vineyard with wine and eatable grapes and fruits orchard. The farmhouse is adjacent to the Makani's forest, at a stone's throw you can find the equipped forest of Baronia, the Maiato one and Montagna Grande, where you can go for healthy walks, picnics and barbecues, collect medicinal and aromatic herbs and you can hand pick wild asparagus and mushrooms in autumn. Moreover, in the owner property there is a vineyard that produces white wine grapes (Grillo) and a vegetable garden. Crossing the Ardignotta wood you can reach the excavations of Monte Polizzo.

Easy to reach

We are a few kilometers away (10Km) from the Segesta Archaeological Sites, 4km from the Monte Polizzo excavations, 30Km from the archaeological site of Selinunte and Erice, 125km from Agrigento. 7Km from the S.Miceli excavations (Early Christian settlements from the IV-V century AD.) And Mokarta (Bronze Age-IV BC), 150km for Agrigento's temples valley.

Unforgettable sea

From the Farmhouse the sea side can be reached easily, it is 35Km away from the sandy beaches of Selinunte and Castellammare, from Castellammare you can also reach the Tonnara of Scopello (sea stacks, this place has been location for movies like the Godfather, Oceans Twelve and many more) and the wonderful coves of the Zingaro's natural protected reserve. From Trapani you can reach by ferry or by hydrofoil, the Egadi's islands. At about 60km there is the pristine beach of San Vito Lo Capo with white sand and crystal blue water.

Anti-covid measures

The bedrooms and common areas are equipped with a hand sanitizer dispenser.
The common areas are equipped with signs for compliance with the safety distances according to current legislation.
The sanitation of the toilets and the support surfaces with disinfectant products is carried out daily in the rooms and several times a day in the common areas according to the influx of users.
Food and drinks are served outdoors in the summer.
The laundry is washed with products and temperatures necessary to eliminate the virus.
A registration of the guests of the restaurant and the living room is carried out and kept for 14 days.
